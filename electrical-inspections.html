Comprehensive Electrical Safety & Diagnostic Inspections

Protect your investment and ensure peace of mind with our detailed, full-system electrical inspections for homes and businesses in Charleston, WV.

Beyond a Simple Checklist: The DRs Designs Difference

Whether you're buying a new home, managing a commercial property, or ensuring your family's safety, a standard visual check isn't enough. Our comprehensive inspection is a deep dive into the health and safety of your entire electrical system.

What's Included in Your Inspection?

  • Main Panel & Sub-Panel Examination: We check for proper breaker sizing, signs of overheating, and correct wiring.
  • Outlet & Switch Testing: We test for correct polarity, grounding, and functionality of all accessible outlets.
  • Wiring Assessment: We identify outdated or hazardous wiring, such as knob-and-tube or failing aluminum.
  • Safety Device Verification: We ensure your smoke detectors, carbon monoxide detectors, and GFCIs are functional and correctly placed.
  • System Load Calculation: We determine if your current system can safely handle its electrical load.

Your Actionable 10-Page Report

Our service culminates in a detailed, easy-to-understand report that empowers you to make informed decisions. This isn't just data; it's a roadmap to a safer property.

Photographic Evidence

Every observation, from minor issues to critical safety hazards, is documented with clear, high-resolution pictures. You see exactly what we see.

Clear Prioritization

We categorize findings into three levels: **Critical Safety Hazards** (Immediate Action Required), **Recommended Repairs** (Future Action Needed), and **System Improvements** (Optional Upgrades).

Plain-Language Explanations

We translate technical jargon into straightforward language, explaining what each issue means and what the potential risks are. No confusion, just clarity.

Costed Solutions

For every recommended repair or upgrade, we provide a transparent, upfront price estimate, allowing you to budget effectively for any necessary work.
